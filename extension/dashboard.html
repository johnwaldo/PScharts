Welcome — let's load your match history
Three steps, about 30–60 seconds total.
1
Log in to PractiScore
The extension reads your match history from your existing PractiScore session — no password is ever shared with this extension.
Open PractiScore →
2
Enter your USPSA member number and name above
Your member number (e.g. A12345) gives the most accurate results. Your name (e.g. Smith, Jane) is used as a fallback. Both are stored locally in your browser only.
3
Click Fetch Scores and wait
The extension opens each match result page in the background and records your score. This takes 30–60 seconds depending on how many matches you have. Don't close the tab.
Optional: also log in to USPSA.org to enable official classifier percentages and your current classification.
